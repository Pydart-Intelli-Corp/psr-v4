## What is an Invoice?

An invoice is your **official receipt** for milk collection. It shows:
- How much milk you supplied
- Quality of your milk (Fat %, SNF %)
- Rate per liter
- **Total amount earned**

## Understanding Your Invoice

### Invoice Number
- Unique ID for each collection
- Format: `INV-[AdminCode]-[Number]`
- Use this for any queries

### Collection Details
- **Date**: When you supplied milk
- **Shift**: Morning or Evening
- **Time**: Exact collection time

### Milk Quality
- **Quantity**: Total liters supplied
- **Fat %**: Fat content in milk
- **SNF %**: Solids-Not-Fat content
- Higher quality = Better rate

## Common Questions

### Q: When will I receive the invoice?
**A**: Immediately after your collection is uploaded to the system by the admin.

### Q: Will I get invoice for every collection?
**A**: Yes, for every new collection. Not for duplicate/updated records.

### Q: What if I don't receive the invoice?
**A**: Check:
1. Email address is correct in farmer registration
2. Check spam/junk folder
3. Contact your society admin

### Q: Can I get invoice for past collections?
**A**: Currently, invoices are generated only for new collections. Contact admin for historical invoices.

### Q: How long should I keep invoices?
**A**: Keep for at least 1 year for payment verification and tax purposes.

### Q: Is the invoice legally valid?
**A**: Yes, it's a computer-generated invoice with all required details.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Not Receiving Invoices

**Solution**:
1. **Check Email**: Verify your email address with admin
2. **Check Spam**: Look in spam/junk folder
3. **Whitelist**: Add sender email to contacts
4. **Contact Admin**: Report if still not receiving

### Problem: Can't Open PDF

**Solution**:
1. **Download First**: Don't try to open from email preview
2. **Use PDF Reader**: Install Adobe Reader or similar
3. **Check Download**: Ensure PDF downloaded completely
4. **Try Different Device**: Open on phone/computer

### Problem: Wrong Details on Invoice

**Solution**:
1. **Check Collection Data**: Verify at time of collection
2. **Contact Admin**: Report discrepancies immediately
3. **Keep Copy**: Save email as evidence
